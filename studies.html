TEPOP Study

Telehealth for Pregnancy or Postpartum

Depression and anxiety during and after pregnancy can lead to health problems for the mother and/or baby. This study explores one possible way to reduce symptoms - a virtual support group that teaches skills on how to improve mental health.

Our research team hopes to learn if the virtual groups, conducted using a secure telehealth platform, are effective in reducing symptoms. We also want to understand if telehealth groups are more accessible to women than in-person groups, since women are able to attend from the comfort of their own home.
